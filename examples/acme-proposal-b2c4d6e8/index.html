The Problem

Spreadsheets and Radio Check-Ins Don't Scale

Your fleet has outgrown manual tracking. Without real-time visibility, maintenance is reactive, driver coaching is guesswork, and compliance documentation eats hours every week.

× No real-time fleet visibility — dispatch can't see where vehicles are without calling drivers
× Manual driver check-ins by phone or radio create gaps and delays in ops awareness
× Maintenance scheduled by calendar date, not actual mileage or engine data — leading to missed services and breakdowns
× No driver performance data — harsh braking, speeding, and idling go unaddressed until incidents occur
× Route inefficiencies invisible without trip replay and on-road data to compare against planned routes
× Compliance reports assembled manually from driver logs — hours of reconciliation per week
× No alert engine — critical thresholds (low fuel, geofence breach, overdue maintenance) go unnoticed until too late
× Fleet data siloed across telematics hardware, paper logs, and disconnected spreadsheets with no unified view

What You Get

Real-Time Fleet Map

Live GPS positions for every vehicle updated every 30 seconds. Dispatch sees status, speed, and last-known location on a single map. Geofence zones trigger automatic alerts on entry and exit.

Predictive Maintenance

Maintenance triggered by engine hours, mileage, and diagnostic fault codes, not calendar dates. Service reminders pushed to fleet managers 14 days out. Full service history log per vehicle.

Driver Performance Dashboard

Per-driver scoring on speeding, harsh braking, rapid acceleration, and idle time. Weekly scorecards for coaching conversations. Trend lines show improvement over time.

Route Optimization

Planned vs. actual route comparison with deviation flagging. Trip replay for any historical route. Mileage and fuel cost analysis by route, driver, and vehicle class.

Compliance Reporting

Automated HOS and mileage reports exportable to PDF or CSV. Inspection checklists with digital sign-off. Audit trail for every trip, including driver ID and vehicle assignment.

Alert Engine

Configurable thresholds for fuel level, geofence, idle duration, speed, maintenance overdue, and fault codes. Alerts route to the right person by role: dispatch, fleet manager, or driver.

Delivery Phases

Discovery, workflow mapping, and hardware integration specs are complete. The phases below cover production development, testing, and go-live deployment.

Phase 1: Core Telemetry 8 SP — 2 weeks ▼
  • Telematics hardware integration (OBD-II and GPS ingestion pipeline)
  • Real-time vehicle position feed with 30-second refresh interval
  • Live fleet map with vehicle status indicators (moving, idle, stopped, offline)
  • Geofence zone builder with entry/exit event logging
  • Vehicle and driver profile management with assignment tracking
  • Basic trip recording: start, stop, distance, duration, fuel consumed
  • Admin dashboard with fleet health overview
  • Role-based access for dispatch, fleet manager, and driver roles
Phase 2: Dashboards & Alerts 12 SP — 3 weeks ▼
  • Driver performance scoring engine (speeding, harsh braking, rapid acceleration, idle time, seatbelt)
  • Per-driver weekly scorecard with trend lines and benchmark comparison
  • Route optimization: planned vs. actual comparison with deviation flagging
  • Trip replay with full GPS breadcrumb trail and event markers
  • Configurable alert engine: thresholds by metric, vehicle, and driver
  • Alert routing rules by role (dispatch, fleet manager, driver SMS/email)
  • Predictive maintenance scheduler linked to mileage, engine hours, and fault codes
  • Maintenance history log per vehicle with service record attachments
  • Fuel consumption analysis by route, driver, and vehicle class
Phase 3: Intelligence Layer 10 SP — 2 weeks ▼
  • Compliance report generator: HOS logs, mileage reports, inspection records

  • Anomaly detection: flag unusual patterns in driver behavior or vehicle diagnostics

  • API endpoint for integration with dispatch and ERP systems

Each phase ships to a staging environment for your team to review before production deployment. Phases run sequentially with core telemetry and live map first, matching your priority for dispatch visibility.
