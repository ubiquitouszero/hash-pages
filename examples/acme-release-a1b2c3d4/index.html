Platform Release v2.4 delivers the most significant upgrade to the Acme warehouse automation system since launch. Core Features

📡

Fleet Coordination Dashboard

Live map of every robot in the facility — location, status, task, and battery level at a glance. Operations managers see bottlenecks forming before they impact throughput.

🚨

Predictive Maintenance Alerts

Sensor data and usage patterns flag units likely to need service 48–72 hours in advance. Schedule maintenance on your terms, not in response to a breakdown mid-shift.

🗺

Zone-Based Routing

Define traffic zones by priority, capacity, and time window. The routing engine respects congestion rules automatically — no manual rerouting during peak pick hours.

🔋

Battery Optimization

Intelligent charge scheduling staggers units to prevent simultaneous charging bottlenecks. Fleet battery reserves stay above threshold through full-shift operation.

📋

Shift Handoff Reports

Auto-generated end-of-shift summaries covering tasks completed, exceptions flagged, and units requiring attention. Incoming supervisors are briefed in 60 seconds.

🔗

Integration Hub

Pre-built connectors for SAP, Manhattan Associates WMS, and major charging station vendors. Bidirectional data sync keeps inventory, task queues, and fleet status aligned.

Fleet Health Score

Every facility receives a Fleet Health Score updated in real time — a composite of uptime, task completion rate, maintenance compliance, and routing efficiency. Operations teams use it to benchmark performance week-over-week and catch degradation before it becomes a problem.

Score RangeHealth LevelOperational Action
90–100ExcellentFleet fully optimized, no intervention needed
75–89GoodMinor inefficiencies present, monitor trends
55–74At RiskDegraded performance, schedule an ops review
<55CriticalSignificant issues, escalate to site manager
How it's calculated: Fleet Health Score weighs four factors — uptime (35%), task completion rate (30%), maintenance compliance (20%), and routing efficiency (15%). Each factor is scored independently so your team knows exactly where to focus.
